The Challenge

The Validation Evidence Problem

Defense program managers and system integrators face a persistent, unsolved problem: certification evidence is fragmented, readiness is opaque, and verification cycles are slow.

■

Evidence is Scattered

PDFs, Word documents, Jira tickets, Excel sheets, Confluence pages, and test reports — no single source of truth. Traceability from requirement to validated evidence is manual, error-prone, and incomplete.

▲

Readiness is Unknown

No automated way to answer the critical questions: Are we ready to operate? What validation is missing? What has failed? What is the mission impact of each unresolved gap?

⌛

Certification is Slow

Manual evidence collection delays program milestones, increases cost, and introduces risk. Defence certification cycles that should take weeks take months — because no tool closes the gap between engineering artefacts and assurance decisions.

The Solution

True Structure Mission Assurance Platform

A deterministic, AI-assisted platform that ingests validation artefacts, traces them to requirements, detects gaps, and produces an explainable mission readiness score.

Requirement Intelligence
AI-assisted extraction and traceability from natural-language documents. Maps requirements to validation evidence with human-in-the-loop review.
Gap Detection
7 deterministic rules identify missing tests, absent evidence, and failed validations. No ambiguity — each gap has a classification, severity, and mission impact statement.
Mission Impact Engine
Translates engineering gaps into operational and mission-level consequences. Connects a failed test result to a degraded mission capability.
Readiness Score
Weighted formula with 6 score caps produces an explainable TRL readiness score. Every point is traceable to a specific gap, evidence item, or validation result.

Demonstration

5-Minute DIANA Demonstration

A walk-through of how True Structure exposes a critical validation gap and produces a mission readiness decision — in under five minutes.

1
Open Dashboard
Platform loads with current programme state. Overall readiness: 61%. Three active gaps flagged.
2
Open Requirements — REQ-003: GPS Recovery
Requirement states: system shall recover GPS lock within 30 seconds of signal denial. Linked to ATVP validation scenario GPS-DENIAL-001.
3
View Linked ATVP Validation Evidence
ATVP engine ran GPS denial scenario. Validation report attached. Evidence chain: requirement → test scenario → execution log → result.
4
See Failed Validation: GPS Recovery Time
Expected: 30s  |  Actual: 48s  |  Result: FAIL
5
Gap Detected: Critical Validation Gap
Gap classification: FAILED_VALIDATION. Severity: CRITICAL. Gap Detection Rule 3 triggered. Mission impact computed automatically.
6
Readiness Impact: 82% → 61%
Score cap applied: CRITICAL gap caps readiness at 65%. Score recalculated. Delta: −21 points. All impact factors displayed with weighting.
7
Generate Report: Mission Not Ready
One-click report export. Decision: MISSION NOT READY. Report includes full evidence chain, gap register, and recommended remediation actions.
